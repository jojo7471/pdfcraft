Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes. All processing happens locally in your browser. Files are never uploaded to any server. We can't see your documents.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely. There are no contracts or commitments. Cancel your subscription anytime from your Stripe account dashboard.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit cards, debit cards, and Apple Pay / Google Pay through Stripe.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied, email us and we'll issue a full refund.

What happens when my free limit is reached?

You'll see a friendly prompt to upgrade. You can wait until the next day for your limits to reset, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited access.

Can I use the API without a server?

The API is designed for server-to-server calls. Pro and Team plans include API keys you can use from any backend or serverless function.
