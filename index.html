Why PDFCraft?

PDFCraft is a free, browser-based PDF toolkit that lets you merge, split, compress, and convert PDF files without uploading them to any server. All processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript, ensuring your documents stay private. Whether you need to combine multiple PDFs into one, extract specific pages, reduce file size, or convert between images and PDFs, PDFCraft handles it all instantly. No sign-up required. No watermarks. No limits on the free tier.
